How does this work?

Writing stories can be fun. But writing stories with total strangers is way better!


This is the deal:

  1. Find a story.
    Make sure you are logged in (or register to join the fun!), scroll to the bottom of the story and write away!
  2. Start your own story.
    Make sure you are logged in, click "add" in the menu, add your piece of the story, click the "Add" button and you're done!

Now the waiting starts... How will the story unfold?
